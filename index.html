Our Mission

Care, consistency, and confidence — built into every word.

We give children a safe, encouraging way to practice communication more often, while supporting the parents, therapists, and researchers who help them grow.

Practice that feels safe

Children repeat sounds, words, rhythm, and tone with no pressure and no judgment.

A bridge between sessions

The goal is to reinforce therapy work at home — never to replace the human care behind it.

Progress worth celebrating

Every attempt matters. Every word is a step toward stronger communication.

The Gap

The time between sessions is where support fades.

Therapy is powerful — but families often go days or weeks between appointments. Together We Talk is designed for that quiet in-between, where gentle daily repetition helps new skills truly stick.

The Companion

A small friend for meaningful speech practice.

We're developing a friendly, approachable robot that uses AI to guide exercises, model conversational rhythm, and make practice feel natural in a child's daily routine.

  • ✓Patient & encouraging — it waits, listens, and celebrates every try.
  • ✓Designed for autism — predictable, calm, and sensory-friendly by design.
  • ✓Parent-aware — gives families simple ways to support without the guesswork.

Built around care, consistency, and confidence.

Our mission is simple: give children a safe and encouraging way to practice communication more often, while supporting the parents, therapists, and researchers who help them grow.

1

Practice that feels safe

Children can repeat sounds, words, rhythm, and tone without pressure or judgment.

2

A bridge between sessions

The goal is to reinforce therapy work at home, not replace the human care behind it.

3

Progress worth celebrating

Every attempt matters. Every word is a step toward stronger communication.

The gap between sessions is where support can fade.

Speech therapy is powerful, but families may go days or weeks between appointments. Together We Talk is designed for that in-between space: the quiet moments at home where gentle repetition can help skills stick.

A small companion for meaningful speech practice.

We are developing a friendly, stylish robot experience that uses AI to guide speech exercises, model conversational rhythm, and make practice feel more natural in a child’s daily routine.

Together We Talk

Empowering communication through compassion, technology, and design.

Our Mission

At Together We Talk, we envision a world where every child has the opportunity to communicate confidently. We’re building intelligent, empathetic technology to support autistic children in developing speech and connection. bridging the space between therapy and daily life.

Our Vision

We’re creating a small, elegant AI-powered robot that becomes a child’s conversational companion. It helps children practice speech exercises, learn tone and rhythm, and develop comfort communicating — all in a safe, encouraging way that fits naturally into their world.

Why It Matters

Speech therapy sessions can be weeks apart. During those long intervals, children often lose progress without consistent practice. Our goal is to fill that space. Creating a bridge that connects sessions, reinforces learning, and celebrates every word spoken.

Connect With Us

Together, we can make communication more accessible and meaningful. Whether you’re a parent, therapist, or researcher, we’d love to hear from you.
